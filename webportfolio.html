Designs

Album Cover

 

This was created as a theoretical album cover for a theoretical band. I had an assignment to make an album cover for a fake band and provide background to my inspiration. The theoretical band is called Nik Naks and they were supposed to be a sort of punk rock band that uses electronic and techy music. I decided to make everything look techno by making the person in the center look polygonal and having the background digitized. Both fonts were acquired in dafont.com under the techno category.

Graphic for Advertising

 

This is a graphic that was created to advertise a show that my class was doing. This show was called Facing the Refugee Crisis, and was created with the intent to promote awareness for the Syrian refugee crisis. In order to invite people to the show, I created an online graphic that was used on my institute's Facebook page. This graphic was used to promote the show and explain where and at what time the show would take place. The graphic at the center was the logo for the show which I designed as well.

Character Design

 

This character was created as concept art for a video game. The character is supposed to represent a male, middle-aged farmer. He loves to wear clothing with checkerboard and plaid patterns. He is tall and skinny because he burns more calories than he consumes. I used a Wacom Intuos Pro tablet to sketch out this character. This character was created in about 15 minutes with the intention of transferring the sketch to Maya in order to begin modeling.
